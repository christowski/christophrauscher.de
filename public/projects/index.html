My design work

From making books to building websites

  • Zeit Online Design

    For the past years, I’ve been responsible for the design of Zeit Online, Germany’s renowned news site.

  • Die Treuhand und die Folgen

    Designed a vast data portal about the Treuhand and the paths of East German companies after the German reunification. A timeline, an interactive map and an object calague make the research accessible. Grimme Online Award Nominee 2024.

  • Die Verkrempelung der Welt

    I am illustrating Gabriel Yoran’s Krautreporter column on the question why products get worse and worse, while they actually could get better.

  • Berlinale Talents 2021

    For the film festival Berlinale Talents, I created the 2021 edition’s key visuals, running under the topic of DREAMS.

  • Village One Cooperative

    In 2022, I co-founded the design and tech studio Village One. As a worker-owned cooperative, we build digital tools and infrastructure. My role: All things design!

  • Sovereign Tech Fund

    As Village One, we are working with the Sovereign Tech Fund on their brand and website, to strengthen Open Source software infrastructure.

  • Warum heißt es Traum und nicht Memoryschaum?

    Gabriel Yoran’s fun word twists, illusrated by me, reached ‛Top 50 Gift Books for Singles’ on Amazon!

  • Zeit Online – Wochenmarkt

    The weekly column with recipies from the printed ZEITmagazin is known and loved. We built its digital companion.

  • 66 Gebote

    66 illustrations for Torsten Groth’s book on system theory in management (Carl Auer Verlag).

  • Zeit Online: Digital Anxieties

    For Zeit Online, I art-directed a photo series on digital anxieties. Photography by Ragnar Schmuck

  • DOK Leipzig – Tone of Voice

    I developed a new tone of voice and writing guidelines for the film festival website’s relaunch. Brand and website design by STATE

  • Kreuzfahrt durch die Republik

    I designed the book cover for Lorenz Meyer’s satirical collection of celeb impersonations.

  • Third Wave Berlin – Agency Branding

    The Berlin based studio for future research and technology contacted me for a brand refresh and website design. Branding, Web Design

  • WDR future report: Synthetic Media

    I designed the future report on Synthetic Media for the WDR innovation hub.

  • Why How What – Company Report

    For a company’s internal management meetup, I teamed up with STATE to create a small book about the event.

  • Disentchanting Invisible Machines

    My master thesis explores the invisible computer from a design-theoretical perspective.
