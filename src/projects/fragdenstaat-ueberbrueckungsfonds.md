    <p><a href="https://fragdenstaat.de" target="_blank">FragDenStaat</a>, the NGO run by the Open Knowledge Foundation Germany, is initiating the Überbrückungsfonds (“bridging fund”). It helps organizations that are waiting for government funds due to late payments—a real existential threat to small teams and projects.</p>
    <p>Together with their team I created a small and dynamic visual brand as well as an informative website and social assets to collect funds.</p>
